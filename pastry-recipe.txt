Method

1.Sift the flour into a large bowl, add the butter and rub in with your fingertips until the mixture resembles fine 
breadcrumbs.
2.Stir in the salt, then add 2-3 tbsp water and mix to a firm dough. Knead the dough briefly and gently on a floured 
surface. Wrap in cling film and chill while preparing the filling
